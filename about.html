About Yo Soy STEM

Mission, HSI Designation, and our dedicated team at San Diego City College.

What is Yo Soy STEM?

Yo Soy STEM is a Title III Hispanic-Serving Institution (HSI) STEM Grant program designed to expand educational opportunities for Hispanic and low-income students pursuing STEM degrees at San Diego City College.

The program aims to:

  • Provide work-based learning experiences
  • Enhance academic success and program completion
  • Provide integrated student support services

San Diego City College — Formally Designated HSI

San Diego City College holds formal HSI status from the U.S. Department of Education, enabling access to the Yo Soy STEM grant focused on serving Hispanic and low-income students in STEM fields.
